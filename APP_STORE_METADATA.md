## App Privacy / Data Use Disclosure

**Data linked to identity:**
- Email (sign-in) — for account management
- User content (birth data: date, time, place) — for personalization

**Data NOT linked to identity:**
- Diagnostics (anonymous app metrics) — for product improvement

**Data NOT collected:**
- Health
- Financial info (Stripe handles this; no card data ever touches our servers)
- Contacts
- Location
- Browsing history
- Search history
- Identifiers other than email
- Purchase history (besides subscription state)
- Sensitive info (race, religion, sexual orientation, etc.)

**Tracking:** None. NSPrivacyTracking=false in PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy. No third-party SDKs that track.
